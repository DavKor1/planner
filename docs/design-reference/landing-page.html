What it does

Your scattered inputs, scheduled into one place.

Most planning lives in a dozen files and your head. Planner pulls every task, deadline, meeting, and recurring commitment out of your documents and places it on a single calendar you actually trust.

↑

Upload anything

Spreadsheets, meeting notes, PDFs, calendar exports, even a photo of a whiteboard. Up to 20 files at once.

✦

AI extraction

The model reads the actual contents and lifts only what's really there — no invented filler, no padding.

↻

Recurrence-aware

"Twice a week for six weeks" becomes the right set of events, spread out and de-overlapped automatically.

⊕

Always additive

New documents merge into your existing plan. Add more anytime — nothing you've built is ever lost.

◷

Day / week / month

Switch views to plan at the right altitude, from a single packed day to a whole quarter.

⌖

Traceable

Every item shows exactly which file it came from and why it was scheduled. Hover to see the reasoning.

How it works

Three steps to a finished schedule.

No setup, no templates, no learning curve. Open Planner to an empty calendar and feed it your material.

STEP 01

Drop your files

Drag in spreadsheets, notes, PDFs, or images. Stage as many as you like, then hit extract.

STEP 02

Watch it extract

Planner reads each file, pulls out every real task and event, and resolves recurring rules live.

STEP 03

Review your calendar

Items land color-coded and dated. Add more documents whenever — it all merges into one plan.
